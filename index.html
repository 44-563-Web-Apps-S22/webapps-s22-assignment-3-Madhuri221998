My favorite place to buy food is Paradise biryani

This biryani is cooked by dum style where the chicken and rice are let to cook
together by placing an airtight lid to the vessel. This is one of the easiest recipe ever.
Instead of using fried onions, I microwaved the onions for 20-30 minutes until they caramelized.
This saves a lot of calories and oil.
